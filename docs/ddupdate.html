Durham and Duke Update

Jacob Ford DCHC MPO

3/3/2021

Using updated data for both Durham and Duke, both parking spaces and ultimately capacity were recalculated for Durham and Duke University.

Using the Downtown Durham Parking Study 2018 total parking data and capacity was collected. For Duke University, a 2015 inventory of parking spaces was used to update the base year. Future projections are based off employment growth and the distribution from the Visitor category to trip purposes is based off peak trip attraction share, mirroring the previous methodology.

Methodology

The Downtown Durham parking Study (DDPS) only looked at a portion of the Durham PASA area.

DDPS Study Area, page 6

Total parking spaces from the DDPS equalled 10,637 after factoring out residential trips and only including those spots within the Durham PASA. Total Parking Uses equalled 15,332, or when doubled equals total capacity of 30,664.
